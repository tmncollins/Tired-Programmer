GDPR Compliance & Data Ethics

AlmaReach is built with privacy, transparency, and institutional trust at its core. We design our data enrichment and analysis processes to align with GDPR principles and best practices for ethical data use.

No Personal Data Stored After Processing

AlmaReach does not retain personal data after enrichment or analysis is completed. Data provided by our clients is processed securely for the sole purpose of delivering agreed insights and is then permanently deleted in accordance with strict data minimization principles.


Publicly Available Information Only

All enrichment performed by AlmaReach relies exclusively on publicly available information. We do not use private databases, scraped credentials, or restricted-access sources.


Transparent Sources & Explainable Analysis

We believe institutions should understand how insights are created. For this reason:


  • All data sources used for enrichment are documented and disclosed
  • Analytical methods and scoring logic are clearly explained
  • No opaque or unexplainable “black-box” decision-making is used

Your Rights Under GDPR

Individuals have the right to object to data enrichment and processing. AlmaReach fully respects these rights and provides clear mechanisms to request restrictions.

Request Blocking of Data Enrichment Request Blocking of Data Processing

Requests are handled promptly and in accordance with applicable data protection laws. AlmaReach may request minimal information necessary to verify the request.
