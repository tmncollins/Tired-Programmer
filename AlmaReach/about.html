Our Story

AlmaReach was born out of frustration and conviction.

Working inside Development Offices, we saw firsthand how ambitious fundraising strategies were being held back by one persistent constraint: incomplete, outdated, and fragmented data. Employment records were years out of date. Alumni had moved industries, founded companies, risen into leadership roles, yet none of it was reflected in the data we relied on.

Routine prospecting tasks became hours of manual detective work. Entire cohorts of alumni were effectively invisible. Not because anyone had failed, but because at an institutional scale, tracking tens or hundreds of thousands of individuals manually is simply impossible. Even the most skilled researchers cannot keep pace with the speed of modern careers.

And without comprehensive current data, critical opportunities were missed. Major gift prospects went unidentified. Potential mentors remained undiscovered. Alumni networks appeared smaller and less dynamic than they truly were. Extraordinary individuals were flying under the radar. We kept asking a simple question: Why can’t Development teams work from a complete, continuously updated dataset?

We searched for a solution. None existed. So we built one.

Today, Development teams are navigating increasingly complex campaigns whilst relying on fragmented enrichment tools, static datasets, and opaque scoring systems. AlmaReach replaces that patchwork approach with a unified, automated platform powered by modern technology and advanced AI. We conduct deeper, broader, and more systematic searches across data sources than any other solution on the market, delivering transparent and comprehensive enrichment across entire alumni bases.

AlmaReach isn’t just another tool, it’s a new foundation for institutional intelligence.
