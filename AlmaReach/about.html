Our Mission

We believe in harnessing modern technology to empower development teams with the data and insights they need to succeed.

Coming from academic and scientific backgrounds, we firmly believe that institutional data retrieval and analysis deserves the same precision and integrity that is applied in academic research. Every component of our system is designed to meet the standards expected by top-tier universities, ensuring that our clients can trust the insights we provide and use them with confidence in fundraising efforts.

Our Story

We founded AlmaReach to bring the operations of Development Offices in line with modern technology and combat the biggest limiting factor in development: good data.

Working in Development we discovered that the data, in particular relating to employment, both past and current, was often long out of date, or even missing entirely, from our alumni databases.

So many times we were performing routine tasks, but found ourselves having to spend hours on end manually sorting through those records which were out of date, and frustrated in the knowledge that there were far more alumni out there that we couldn't access. This is the fault of no individual: the fact is, that in dealing with thousands to hundreds of thousands of alumni, it would be genuinely impossible to keep track of so many, however skilled the researcher. But without this data, many of our efforts were stifled: we struggled with identifying major gift prospects, finding mentors for students, and even knowing where our alumni were based in many cases. A huge number of people who were interesting for a range of reasons were flying under the radar.

We found ourselves asking 'Why? Why can't we use a full, updated dataset'? But despite extensive searches, we found no available produce to provide this. So we decided to create our own.

Development teams are navigating increasingly complex campaigns, whilst relying on fragmented data enrichment tools and opaque scoring systems. We have built AlmaReach as the solution, providing a comprehensive and transparent alternative for mass data enrichment of entire alumni bases, using a more comprehensive search of data sources than any other tool on the market.

What Distinguishes AlmaReach

Scale


Unlike manual researchers, we use modern automation tools to provide enriched profiles and insights for many thousands of alumni, ensuring that no stone goes left unturned in the Advancement pipeline.

Transparency


All scoring frameworks and modelling approaches are clearly documented. With no black-box outputs, institutions can interpret exactly what out outputs mean for their fundraising efforts.

Privacy


We recognise that when being entrusted with such personal data, privacy is paramount. We are committed to upholding GDPR best practice, and to relying totally on fully public data sources.
