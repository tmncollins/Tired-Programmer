Trusted Intelligence for Institutional Advancement

AlmaReach partners with leading academic institutions to deliver transparent analytics, ethical data enrichment, and strategic insight that strengthens fundraising outcomes.

Our Mission

To provide advancement teams with rigorous, ethical, and actionable alumni intelligence — enabling confident, evidence-led fundraising decisions.

We believe institutional data deserves the same precision and integrity applied in academic research. Every model, enrichment layer, and insight is designed to meet the standards expected by top-tier universities.

Our Story

AlmaReach was founded in response to a clear institutional gap: powerful alumni datasets without structured analytical clarity.

Development teams were navigating increasingly complex campaigns, yet relying on fragmented enrichment tools and opaque scoring systems. We built AlmaReach to provide a more rigorous alternative — one grounded in transparent modelling, public data integrity, and institutional alignment.

Today, we work alongside advancement leaders who demand precision, discretion, and measurable strategic value.

What Distinguishes AlmaReach

Explained Analytics


Clear scoring frameworks and documented modelling approaches. No black-box outputs. Institutions understand exactly how insights are derived.

GDPR Compliant


Built around responsible public data enrichment and compliant processes. Data governance is foundational — not an afterthought.

Institution-First Design


Every feature is built specifically for development strategy, donation identification, and campaign readiness.
