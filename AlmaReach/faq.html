Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about AlmaReach, our data sources, analytics, and compliance practices.

Data Enrichment

AlmaReach uses only publicly available information. We do not access private databases, restricted systems, or credentials-protected content.

Data enrichment involves supplementing existing alumni records with publicly available context such as professional information, geographic indicators, and engagement-relevant signals.

Analysis & Reports

Yes. All enrichment sources are disclosed, and analytical methods and scoring logic are clearly explained. AlmaReach does not rely on opaque or black-box decision making.

Outputs may include scored datasets, segmentation files, summary reports, and interpretation guidance designed to support advancement and alumni engagement decisions.

Pricing

Pricing is based on project scope, data volume, and analytical depth. AlmaReach does not sell data subscriptions or per-record access.

No long-term commitment is required. Institutions may engage AlmaReach on a project or periodic basis.

Data Privacy

No. AlmaReach does not retain personal data after enrichment or analysis is completed. Data is deleted in line with data minimization principles.

Yes. Individuals may request blocking of data enrichment or processing at any time, and such requests are handled promptly.
